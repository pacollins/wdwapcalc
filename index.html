Methodology

Hotel prices based on the cheapest advertised rack rates for 2018 (Source: MouseSavers). DVC Rentals are based on the lowest average price of a week at $14/point.

All prices used within the calculator are the cheapest that I could find (base room accommodations during value times). This is because the more expensive the trip, the more worthwhile an AP becomes. Thus, if it is worthwhile under these conditions, it will be worthwhile regardless of when you go. Feel free to check the prices in the constants tab. If you know of a lower price for any of the items listed, please let me know and I'll adjust it.

Remember, you don't have to get everyone an Annual Pass to get the savings an Annual Pass gets you.
