A calculator to determine the value of purchasing an Annual Pass at Walt Disney World in Orlando, Florida.

Created for r/WaltDisneyWorld

Hotel prices based on advertised rack rates (or renting DVC Points @ $14/pp) compared to the AP discounts for the "Summer Rates" for the first full week of June (Source: MouseSavers). All prices used are the cheapest I can find (base room accommodations). Also, don't forget that not everyone has to have an AP to get the AP discounts. ;D

If prices go up, AP becomes more worthwhile. If prices go down, AP becomes less worthwhile.

Feel free to check the prices in the other two tabs below. If you know of a lower price, please let me know and I'll adjust it.

NOTE: These are estimates based on typical pricing. The only tax that is included is the tax on the hotel.
