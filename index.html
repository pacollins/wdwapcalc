Methodology

Hotel prices are based on the advertised rack rate during a "Value" week in 2020. (Source: MouseSavers). While I acknowledge that discounts are common place, the inclusion of inconsistent discounts would skew the validity of the results. If you have a rate, feel free to utilize the "custom" section. DVC Rentals are based a value week in the smallest room for a resort at $19/point in 2020. (Source: DVC Request).

Additional items, such as tickets, are the cheapest that I could find. This is because the more expensive the trip, the more worthwhile an AP becomes. Thus, if it is worthwhile under these conditions, it wil be worthwhile regardless of when you go. Feel free to check the prices in the constants tab. If you know of a lower price for any of the items listed or have suggestions to improve the calculator, please let me know and I'll adjust it.

Remember, you don't have to get everyone an Annual Pass to get the savings an Annual Pass gets you.
